Concept

Our team has developed an app that encourages the user to develop positive habits. The app is titled "bitzz" and it works by gamifying task completion and rewards users with points for completing these tasks. "bitzz" allows the users to create daily or weekly tasks and assigns points for its completion. A progress bar tracks the user's progress over the day or week depending on the task type. The app also has a social aspect by allowing the user to add friends and create groups. This fosters a positive support environment where other people can encourage each other and adds a little bit of competition. The target demographic for this app is the Millennial generation and younger. This app is designed for a smartphone and the Millennial generation and younger frequently use technology on a regular basis to enhance their lives. We believe this demographic are more apt to use "bitzz" due to their frequent use of smartphone technology. The main purpose of "bitzz" is to develop long-lasting positive habits. Habits are formed when a user completes tasks on a repetitive basis. Positive habits will form from the simple execution of small tasks over a period of time. These tasks can be mundane or tedious which is why people tend to put aside certain tasks for a later time. Gamification makes it fun for the users to complete these mundane tasks and creates a reward system that keeps the users coming back to complete more of these tasks. Eventually, users will begin to create long-term positive habits without even realizing it.
